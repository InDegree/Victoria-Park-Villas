MR HAN LOKE KWANG

HYLA Architects is renowned for their pursuit of design excellence and highly professional service. The Principal, Han Loke Kwang has experience in a wide range of architectural and interior design projects. The firm’s extensive portfolio includes owner-occupied houses, corporate, retail and condominium interiors, healthcare facilities, master planning as well as various institutional buildings. The award-winning firm believes that architecture is about creating spaces for its intended function, in its intended location, for the intended users, setting the stage for a delightful, timeless experience.

WHAT IS UNIQUE ABOUT THE SITE THAT VICTORIA PARK

VILLAS SITS ON? HOW HAS THAT INSPIRED YOUR DESIGN?

The site is unique in its undulating terrain and the possibility of a basement entry. Our approach, therefore, was to take advantage of these unique features. We also aimed to maximise the living areas on the first storey, and offer maximum privacy by means of orientation and screens.

COULD YOU ELABORATE ON THE SPATIAL EXPERIENCE OF

VICTORIA PARK VILLAS? WHAT WERE YOU AIMING FOR?

In designing for Victoria Park Villas, we aimed to maximise the feeling of space and light. For example, we chose lighted colours for the interiors, using oak floors, light grey marble, white timber veneered doors, and white glass kitchen cabinets. The overall effect is a light and bright modern house that would appeal to the most discerning of clients.

WHAT IS UNIQUE ABOUT THE SITE, AND HOW HAS THAT

INSPIRED YOUR DESIGN?

The plot on which the Courtyard Bungalow sits on is a triangulated piece of land perched on a high point. The design of the Courtyard Bungalow, therefore, tries to capitalise on the views over the site. For the Courtyard Bungalow, the site is a long linear plot with a discreet and quiet frontage. In our design, we created intimate courtyards where the living spaces can look into.

MR LIM CHENG KOOI

Founded in 2006, AR43 Architects is an award-winning design firm specialising in a wide range of works from custom-designed residences, hospitality, institutional, and commercial to interior architecture projects all around Asia. The firm is led by founder Lim Cheng Kooi, who has more than 20 years of experience in the field and has received numerous awards for his work, including the 10th SIA Architectural Design Awards 2010. TELL US ABOUT YOUR DESIGN APPROACH TOБ

VICTORIA PARK VILLAS.

The overall planning of Victoria Park Villas emphasizes on creating a home with an abundance of greenery, exclusivity, and privacy. The houses look inward towards a streetscape that is lushly landscaped. Elevated living spaces from the street level exude a sense of exclusiveness and provide utmost privacy for every house owner.

VICTORIA PARK VILLAS? WHAT WERE YOU AIMING FOR?

The spatial attributes of this house come from the lightness that exudes from within. The use of glass and screened facades enables the fluidity of light to sculpt the spaces in the house. In doing so, each of these spaces within the house is filled with ample light that characterises them with warmth and homeliness, while preserving sophistication and luxury.
